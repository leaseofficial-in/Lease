The Problem

Renting in India is still broken

Most landlords run their rentals over WhatsApp, notebooks, and handshake agreements. That leaves everyone unprotected.

🏠 For Landlords

  • No record of property condition at move-in — impossible to claim deposit deductions
  • Chasing rent over WhatsApp every month
  • No centralised record of who paid, when, and how much
  • Repair requests lost in chat threads
  • Rental agreements printed, signed, and promptly lost
  • No proof trail if a dispute goes to court

🏡 For Tenants

  • Deposit deducted unfairly with no way to dispute
  • No HRA receipts for tax exemption (losing thousands/year)
  • Repair requests ignored with no escalation path
  • No digital copy of the rental agreement
  • Rent history lives in UPI apps, not accessible
  • Security completely depends on landlord goodwill
