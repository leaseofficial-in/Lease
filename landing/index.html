What Makes Us Different

Three things no other rental app does

These aren't nice-to-haves. They're the features that prevent the disputes, the losses, and the tax penalties that Indian renters face every year.

📸

Room-by-Room Photo Proof

At move-in, the tenant photographs every room — hall, bedroom, kitchen, bathroom — inside the app. Each photo is room-labelled and time-stamped, tied to the rental record. At move-out, the same process repeats. The two sets of photos sit side by side.

"My tenant claimed the wall was already cracked. I pulled up the move-in photo from 14 months ago. Case closed."
🛡️

Deposit Protection Ledger

Every deposit transaction is on record — amount received, any deductions with reasons, final refund. The tenant can see the full ledger any time. When it's time to move out, there's nothing to fight about because everything was logged when it happened.

"I used to just trust that I'd get my deposit back. Now I have a ledger I can show anyone — including a court if I have to."
🧾

Instant HRA Receipts

The Indian tax code gives tenants an exemption on rent paid — but only if you have receipts with PAN, address, and payment details. Your landlord never gives them. RentyBase generates a compliant PDF receipt in one tap from the tenant's dashboard.

"I saved ₹22,000 in tax this year because I finally had receipts. I didn't even realise I was leaving this money on the table."
🔔

Automatic Rent Reminders

Push notification + WhatsApp reminder 2 days before, on due day, and 1 day overdue. Overdue status escalates automatically.

🔧

Repair Request Tracking

Tenant raises a request with photos. Landlord updates the status. Both sides have a timestamped paper trail.

📋

Digital L&L Agreement

Generates a legally structured Leave & License agreement pre-filled with your rental terms. Stored permanently in the app.

💰

Flexible Payment Logging

UPI, bank transfer, cash, cheque — log any payment method with UTR reference and optional proof screenshot upload.

📊

Full Payment History

Every rent payment, with date, amount, method, and receipt. Accessible any time — useful for visa, loan, and income tax filings.

🏢

PG & Multi-Unit Support

Manage multiple rooms under one property. Each room has its own tenant, rent amount, proof photos, and payment record.

FAQ

Questions we actually get asked

Does my tenant need to download an app to use this?
+
No. The invite link opens in any browser — Chrome, Safari, whatever. Your tenant can sign in with Google and upload photos from the web. If they want the native experience on their phone, they can install the app later. But you can't block anyone from joining by saying "they didn't install the app."
Is it really free? What's the catch?
+
No catch. We're in beta and we want landlords using the product so we can improve it. When we introduce paid tiers, we'll notify you 30 days in advance and you'll never be cut off mid-rental. Beta users will get a better price than new signups.
What happens to my move-in photos? Can they be deleted?
+
Photos are stored on Supabase (secure cloud storage). Once a move-in proof set is submitted by the tenant, it's locked — neither landlord nor tenant can edit or delete individual photos. The set stays attached to the rental record permanently, even after the rental ends.
My tenant paid cash. Can I still log it?
+
Yes. When marking a payment, you choose the method: UPI, bank transfer, cash, or cheque. For UPI and bank transfers you can add the UTR reference number. For cash you can add a note. Everything gets a timestamp and a record in the payment history.
Are the HRA receipts valid for tax filing?
+
The generated PDF includes all required fields for HRA exemption under Section 10(13A): landlord name, PAN number, tenant name, property address, period, amount, and payment method. We recommend you also get your landlord to confirm the PAN is correct in their profile before generating.
What if I have 3 tenants in a PG — can I manage all of them?
+
Yes. When creating a property, choose "PG / Hostel" and you can add multiple units (rooms). Each room gets its own invite link, its own tenant, its own rent amount, and its own proof photos. You manage them all from one landlord dashboard.
