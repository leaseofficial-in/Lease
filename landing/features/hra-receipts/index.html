HRA & Tax — FAQs

Common questions about rent receipts

Under Section 10(13A) of the Income Tax Act 1961, tenants can claim HRA tax exemption only with rent receipts. Landlords are expected to provide them on request. If annual rent exceeds ₹1 lakh, the landlord's PAN is also mandatory on the receipt.

A valid HRA rent receipt must include: tenant's full name, landlord's full name and PAN (if annual rent > ₹1 lakh), complete property address with pin code, the month and year, rent amount in figures and words, date of payment, and landlord's signature.
