We built this for the problems you know too well

Every feature in RentyBase exists because a landlord told us it was a monthly headache.

"Did the rent come in?"

You check your bank app, match the transfer, then text the tenant. Every. Single. Month. RentyBase notifies you the moment rent is paid — with payment proof attached.

"I need HRA receipt for April"

The March rush: 10 tenants ask for 12 months of receipts at once. With RentyBase, tenants download their own receipts — you never have to generate one manually again.

Deposit disputes at move-out

"The bathroom tile was already cracked!" Sound familiar? RentyBase captures move-in photos of every room. No photos, no dispute. The evidence is timestamped and locked.

Repair requests on WhatsApp

Tenant messages get buried in personal chats. You forget. They escalate. RentyBase gives repairs their own thread — tracked, timestamped, with photo evidence.

Managing 3 properties in Excel

Different tabs, different formats, manual entry. RentyBase shows all your properties on one dashboard — rent status, deposits, maintenance — everything in one screen.

Following up on overdue rent

Awkward reminders via WhatsApp. RentyBase sends automatic reminders before and after the due date — professional, non-confrontational, and no longer your job.
