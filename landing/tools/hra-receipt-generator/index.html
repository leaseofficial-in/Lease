FAQ

Common questions

Is a self-generated HRA receipt valid for tax filing? +
The Income Tax Act does not specify who must issue the receipt — only that it must reflect actual rent paid. A receipt generated through a platform like RentyBase, where the landlord's PAN was collected at onboarding and payments are logged, carries significantly more weight than a plain Word document. That said, it is always best to have the landlord sign or acknowledge the receipt. Share the PDF with your landlord over WhatsApp and ask for a "seen" or acknowledgement.
What if my landlord doesn't have a PAN? +
If the landlord genuinely has no PAN, they must provide a signed Form 60 declaration. You submit this along with your HRA claim. Leave the PAN field blank in this generator and note "Form 60 obtained" on the receipt. Your employer's payroll team will guide the specific process.
Can I generate receipts for past months? +
Yes. Select the appropriate month and year using the dropdowns. There is no legal restriction on generating receipts for past periods, as long as the rent was actually paid for those months and the amounts are accurate.
My employer needs 12 receipts — do I generate them one by one? +
With this free tool, yes — you'd generate one per month. On RentyBase, all receipts are generated automatically from the payment records already in the system. Your tenant can download any month's receipt in one tap, any time, without asking the landlord. If you manage your rental on RentyBase, you never need this tool again.

Yes, completely free. No signup, no email required, no credit card. Fill the form, download the PDF. That's it.
